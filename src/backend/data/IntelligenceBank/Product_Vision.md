# Product Vision Document: IntelligenceBank Platform (V3)

## 1. Executive Summary & Elevator Pitch

**For** global enterprise marketing, brand, and legal compliance teams

**Who** struggle with asset sprawl, content bottlenecks, inconsistent brand representation, and compliance risks across multiple regions,

**The IntelligenceBank Platform (V3)** is an Intelligent Content Operations and Digital Asset Management (DAM) ecosystem

**That** provides a centralized, highly secure, and AI-powered environment to create, manage, approve, and distribute digital content at scale.

**Unlike** fragmented legacy cloud storage or siloed project management tools,

**Our Product** seamlessly unifies intuitive "Smart Page" brand portals, automated Asset Intelligence (AI tagging, facial recognition, and vector-based document queries), and enterprise-grade, multi-stage approval workflows into a single, compliant source of truth.

---

## 2. Core Objectives & North Star

Our ultimate goal is to **reduce the friction between content creation and compliant distribution to zero.** **Key Business Objectives:**

1. **Accelerate Time-to-Market:** Reduce content approval cycles through automated routing, bulk-reviewing capabilities, and in-browser markup.
2. **Ensure Brand & Legal Compliance:** Mandate dynamic workflows where content cannot be published or downloaded without the explicit, staged sign-off from necessary departments (e.g., Legal, Brand, localized Marketing).
3. **Drive Asset Discoverability:** Leverage Azure-backed AI to instantly categorize assets via automated image/video tagging and facial recognition.
4. **Empower Self-Service:** Allow non-technical users to build stunning, responsive internal portals ("Smart Pages") to distribute campaigns without requiring IT or developer intervention.

---

## 3. Target User Personas

To build effectively, the Scrum teams must understand the distinct needs of our primary platform users:

- **The Main / Workflow Admin (The Orchestrator):** Needs granular control over platform settings (MCP), the ability to design complex, multi-staged approval templates, and manage user groups/divisions.
- **The Content Requester (The Creator):** Marketing professionals or external agencies uploading batches of files (up to 1,000 via multi-upload fields). They need quick ways to track the status of their pending requests and easily group related assets for review.
- **The Reviewer (The Gatekeeper):** Brand managers or legal counsel. They suffer from review fatigue. They need robust "Bulk Review" screens to approve, decline, or mark up dozens of assets simultaneously, with the ability to dynamically re-assign tasks to collaborators.
- **The Public / Guest User (The External Contributor):** Freelancers or external partners submitting files. They require a frictionless, secure upload process without needing a platform account, backed by mandatory automated virus scanning.

---

## 4. Key Value Pillars (Strategic Focus Areas)

Our product strategy rests on four major functional pillars. All Epic and User Story prioritization should tie back to these pillars:

### Pillar 1: Smart Page Portals (Brand Distribution)

We are transforming from a static repository into a dynamic distribution engine. The V3 release introduces a drag-and-drop Smart Page builder. Users can nest dynamic Widgets (Videos, Images, Text, Buttons, Spacers) inside Sections and Blocks. Crucially, these pages must be instantly responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile, automatically respecting image focal points and aspect ratios.

### Pillar 2: Intelligent Workflows (Content Governance)

Workflows must adapt to the complexity of enterprise organizations. The platform will support "Staged Requests" where approvals pass through sequential departments. To combat bottlenecking, we are introducing a powerful Bulk Review interface capable of handling up to 300 cross-template items simultaneously, and "Grouped Approvals" to process related assets as a single entity.

### Pillar 3: Asset Intelligence (AI & Automation)

We will leverage AI to eliminate manual data entry. The platform will feature automated Azure image and video tagging, alongside a dedicated "Facial Recognition Training Centre" to identify "Known" faces across the ecosystem and flag "Unknown" faces for privacy. Furthermore, the "Ask AI" Alpha feature will introduce natural language, vector-based searching against document text.

### Pillar 4: Enterprise-Grade Processing & Security

As a trusted repository, the system must handle massive data operations invisibly. This includes automatic background processing of rich media (e.g., automatically converting uploaded Lottie and GIF files into highly optimized MP4s for preview rendering), mandatory virus scanning on all public uploads, and robust session-management for authenticated users.

---

## 5. Future Outlook (Beyond V3)

While the immediate focus is on the V3 architecture (Smart Pages, AI Tagging, Bulk Approvals), the platform is evolving toward Predictive Content Operations. In the next 18-24 months, the vision will expand to include:

- Fully generative AI for drafting Smart Page layouts based on prompt inputs.
- Predictive workflow routing (AI predicting the necessary legal reviewers based on OCR scanning of uploaded documents).
- Deep integration of Red Marker compliance checks directly into the primary asset rendering pipeline.
