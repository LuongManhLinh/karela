IntelligenceBank is an AI-powered marketing operations platform centered around Digital Asset Management (DAM), workflow automation, and compliance management. It helps organizations store, organize, review, approve, and distribute marketing content from a single system. Teams can manage assets, run approval workflows, enforce brand guidelines, track audit trails, and automatically detect legal or branding risks using AI. It targets marketing, creative, legal, and compliance teams, especially in regulated industries. Features such as guest uploads, public portals, templates, reporting dashboards, and workflow customization explain many of the user stories you shared.
